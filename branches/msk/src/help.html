Shortcuts

Alt+C
Clears the results
Alt+H
Opens this (the help) page.
Alt+O
Opens the options page.
Alt+P
Opens the popout.
Alt+R
Copies the most recent result to the clipboard.
Alt+S
Copies the most recent result's source to the clipboard.
Ctrl/Command+click
Ctrl/Command+clicking a input, result, or source link copies it to the clipboard.

FAQ

Why don't I see any result when I press "="?
To get a result, you must press Enter. You can add an "=" to the end of an expression, but it is not necessary.
